## Download

Grab **`movx-android.apk`** from the [Releases](https://github.com/Kishanx08/movx/releases) page — download it directly on your phone.

## Install

1. Open the downloaded `movx-android.apk`
2. If prompted, allow installs from this source
3. Tap **Install**
4. Open **MovX** from your app drawer

## FAQ

**Why does Android warn me before installing?**
The app isn't distributed through the Play Store, so Android shows a standard first-run warning for that. It's safe to proceed.

**What Android version do I need?**
Android 7.0 (Nougat) or newer.

**Where do I report a bug?**
Open an [issue](https://github.com/Kishanx08/movx/issues) with your Android version and what happened.
