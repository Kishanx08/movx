## ✨ What is MovX?

MovX is a desktop app version of [movx.me](https://movx.me) — the same streaming experience you already know, packaged into a clean, standalone app for your PC.

The biggest difference? **No ads.**

Streaming sources are notorious for bombarding you with popups, redirects, and sketchy ad injections. MovX ships with an ad blocker built directly into the app, so you get straight to what you actually opened it for — watching.

## 🚀 Why you'll like it

- 🛡️ **Ads blocked automatically** — no extensions to install, no settings to configure. It just works out of the box.
- 🚫 **No popups, no redirects** — the stuff that normally hijacks your browser tab never gets the chance to.
- 🖥️ **Feels like a real app** — its own window, its own icon, no browser tabs or bookmarks bar cluttering your view.
- ⚡ **Fast and lightweight** — opens straight into your content.

## 📥 Download & Install

1. Head to the **[Releases](https://github.com/Kishanx08/movx/releases)** page.
2. Download the latest **`MovX-Setup.exe`**.
3. Run the installer and follow the prompts.
4. Launch **MovX** from your Start Menu or desktop shortcut.

That's it — you're ready to stream.

> **Note:** Windows may show a SmartScreen warning since the app isn't code-signed. Click **More info → Run anyway** to proceed.

## ❓ FAQ

**Is this safe to use?**
Yes — MovX doesn't collect any data or bundle anything beyond the ad blocker. It's a personal project built for a cleaner streaming experience.

**Does it work offline?**
No, an internet connection is required since content is streamed, not downloaded.

**Is this available for Mac or Linux?**
Not currently — only a Windows installer is available right now.

**Something isn't working — where do I report it?**
Open an [issue](https://github.com/Kishanx08/movx/issues) and describe what happened.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

MovX doesn't host any video content itself — it streams from third-party sources. This project is built for personal use; use it responsibly and in line with your local laws.
